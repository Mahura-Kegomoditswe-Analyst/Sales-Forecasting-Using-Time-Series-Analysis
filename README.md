## üìö Introduction

In the modern retail industry, accurate sales forecasting is essential for optimizing **inventory**, improving **supply chain efficiency**, planning **promotional activities**, and stabilizing **revenue**. Retailers with multiple stores often experience unpredictable sales fluctuations, making it difficult to anticipate demand using intuition alone.

This case study examines a real-world scenario where ABC Retail Ltd. seeks to enhance its forecasting capabilities using **structured time series analysis**. Using historical sales data collected over 1,000 days across three stores, this project applies systematic data analytics techniques to uncover trends, seasonality patterns, and external factors influencing sales.

## üõ†Ô∏è Tasks and Analytical Workflow

To address the business problems, the project follows a structured analytic workflow:

### **1. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
- Identify missing values, outliers, and anomalies.  
- Visualize sales trends across stores.  
- Detect seasonality and irregular patterns.

### **2. Understanding Seasonality & Trends**
- Evaluate daily, weekly, and monthly sales cycles.  
- Measure the effect of holidays and weekdays.  
- Analyze promotional impacts on sales uplift.

### **3. Building a Forecasting Model**
- Test suitable models such as **Moving Average**, **ARIMA**, or other time series methods.  
- Train and validate using historical data.  
- Evaluate model accuracy using appropriate metrics.

### **4. Generating Sales Predictions**
- Forecast future sales for each store.  
- Compare forecasted vs. actual sales.  
- Identify key influencers such as promotions and holidays.

### **5. Optimizing Inventory Management**
- Align stock levels with forecasted demand.  
- Reduce excess inventory and avoid stockouts.  
- Enable proactive restocking decisions.

### **6. Enhancing Promotional Strategy**
- Identify which stores benefit most from promotions.  
- Determine optimal timing and frequency.  
- Evaluate past campaign performance.

### **7. Reporting & Business Decision-Making**
- Communicate findings to key stakeholders.  
- Build dashboards for real-time forecasting insights.  
- Provide recommendations for operational improvement.
